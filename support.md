## What is Vaultlens?

Vaultlens inspects your Obsidian vaults stored in iCloud Drive — surfacing vault health, structure, activity, and AI-agent-editing signals — **without opening Obsidian** and **without your files ever leaving your device**.

## Frequently asked questions

**How do I add a vault?**
Tap **＋** on the Vaults screen and pick your vault's folder (usually under iCloud Drive → Obsidian). Vaultlens remembers it securely for next time.

**Why does it need access to my files?**
To read file metadata (and, for deep-scan features, note text) so it can compute your vault's stats. It only ever accesses folders you explicitly pick.

**Does my data leave my device?**
No. Vaultlens has no network connection at all — no servers, no analytics, no uploads. Everything is computed locally. See the Privacy Policy.

**What's included in Pro?**
The free tier gives you a single-vault metadata dashboard. **Pro** unlocks multi-vault comparison, Home Screen & Lock Screen widgets, the deep-scan link engine and graph, agent-signal detection, the vault health score, and Shortcuts/App Intents.

**How do I manage or cancel my subscription?**
Subscriptions are managed by Apple: **Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions**, or in Vaultlens under **Settings → Manage Subscription**. Cancel anytime; access continues until the end of the paid period.

**I paid but Pro isn't showing / I got a new phone.**
Open **Settings → Restore Purchases** in Vaultlens. This re-checks your active subscription with Apple.

**My widget isn't updating.**
Open the app and let it rescan the pinned vault — widgets refresh from the latest in-app scan. Make sure the vault is still accessible in iCloud.

**A vault shows "lost access" or won't scan.**
iCloud occasionally invalidates a folder bookmark (e.g. after moving the folder). Remove and re-add the vault to re-grant access.
